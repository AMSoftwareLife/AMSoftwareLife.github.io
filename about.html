About AM Software Life

We Are Building The Future

AM Software Life is a campaign launched by AM Software inspired by Andrés Montenegro’s life experience of struggle, resilience and perseverance in the face of disaster and his selfless altruistic pursuit of global empowerment through technology, working tirelessly to achieve his vision of “Building The Future” by bridging the gaps that exist in today’s digital world, without any financial remuneration for years, investing all his life savings in this inspiring pursuit, which to this day has not been stopped even by the harshest of crises.

After spending years trying to fund these endeavours with the minimal revenue generated by AM Software while continuing development under financial disaster, AM Software Life will have a real-life positive impact in the world by providing FREE website development services for other projects that also strive to make the world a more fulfilling and inspiring place.

AM Software Life will develop 100 FREE websites per year, delivering 2 FREE websites per week, with 3-years FREE web hosting included for each of them.

Starting on October 2020, AM Software Life will have provided 100 FREE websites to worthwhile inspiring projects/organizations around the world by October 2021.

Furthermore, AM Software Life is teaching completely free Computer Science and Programming courses to youths in Africa and the Caribbean to provide them with powerful skills and tools that will open the doors to a brighter future for them, their families and countries, and we are ready to scale up this initiative to reach more young african and caribbean people, once the remaining funding goal is achieved.

Of course, this is a completely altruistic endeavor with very considerable costs and is self-funded. If you wish to support this campaign and help us achieve our vision, you can donate via PayPal or Bitcoin or support us monthly on Patreon in exchange of exclusive benefits. Every donation and contribution is highly valued.
